Understanding Le Chéile is a social enterprise led by a dedicated team of students of National University of Ireland, Galway. It was founded in 2017 by Enactus Team Leader, Katie Nolan and is currently powered by Enactus NUI Galway. Le Chéile conducts workshops with the aim of teaching a fundamental awareness to Businesses and Second Level Education Institutes around Ireland. With our interactive and thought-provoking workshops, students and employees alike will leave with a better understanding of autism and comfortably know how to interact and support autistic people in the workplace and society.
